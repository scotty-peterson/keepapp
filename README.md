# Keep Up

Stay connected with the people who matter.

## Features

- **Track People**: Add friends, family, and colleagues you want to stay in touch with
- **Set Frequencies**: Choose how often you want to reach out (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- **Visual Status**: Instantly see who you're overdue to contact
- **Track Commitments**: Never forget promises you've made to people
- **Local Storage**: Your data stays private on your device

## Problem This Solves

We all have good intentions to stay connected with the people we care about, but life gets busy. This app helps you:

1. Remember to reach out to friends you haven't talked to in a while
2. Keep track of commitments you've made so you can follow through
3. Maintain relationships without letting them fade due to neglect

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Local Storage for data persistence
- No backend required - your data stays on your device

## Deployment

This app is deployed on Vercel. Simply connect your GitHub repo to Vercel for automatic deployments.
